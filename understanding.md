# Website Understanding: AL Gable ALAkhter Company (شركة الجبل الأخضر)

## Overview
**AL Gable ALAkhter Company For Livestock And Meat** (Arabic: **شركة الجبل الأخضر لاستيراد المواشي واللحوم**) is a prominent Libyan limited liability company specializing in the import, breeding, and sale of live animals. The name "AL Gable ALAkhter" is a transliteration of "Al-Jabal Al-Akhdar" meaning "The Green Mountain".

## Core Business
- **Primary Activity**: Importing and selling live animals globally.
- **Main Products**:
  - **Sheep**: Top quality, including Libyan national sheep and imports from Sudan and Chad.
  - **Bulls**: Imported and bred.
  - **Camels**: Imported from Sudan and Chad.

## History & Leadership
- **Founders**: Miftah and Jibril Faraj Jabran (Barkash) - (مفتاح وجبريل فرج جبران - بركش).
- **Origin**: Misrata, Libya.
- **Experience**: The founders have been in the livestock business since **1985**.
- **Establishment**: The current company was formally established in **2005**.

## Infrastructure & Scale
- **Capacity**: The company maintains well-equipped pens capable of holding:
  - **25,000 Bulls**
  - **100,000 Sheep**
- **Global Network**: They import from a wide range of countries including:
  - **Europe**: Romania, Ukraine, Spain, Croatia, Ireland, Czech Republic, Hungary, Moldova, Portugal.
  - **Other**: Australia, Brazil.

## Associate Entities (الشركات التابعة)
The group includes several specialized companies:
- **ALHILAL ALDAWLY COMPANY** (شركة الهلال الدولي لاستيراد المواشي واللحوم)
- **MAJMOET ALMAWASHI ALJADIDA COMPANY** (شركة مجموعة المواشي الجديدة لاستيراد المواشي واللحوم)
- **AL MOTHETA AL MAHELEVA COMPANY** (شركة المتحدة المحلية لاستيراد المواشي واللحوم)
